Privacy Policy – MyCalc

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

MyCalc is a calculator application developed by MyDreams. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data MyCalc collects — and more importantly, what it does not collect.

Data Collection

MyCalc does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • No account or registration is required.
  • No personal information is ever collected.
  • No usage data, analytics, or telemetry is sent to any server.
  • No advertising network or third-party SDK is included.
  • The app does not require an internet connection.

Local Storage

MyCalc stores your preferences (theme, custom button layouts, calculation history) locally on your device using Android's standard storage mechanisms. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the app.

Permissions

MyCalc does not request any sensitive permissions (camera, microphone, location, contacts, etc.).

Children's Privacy

MyCalc does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the new version will be published at this URL with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you may contact us at the email address listed on the Google Play Store page.
